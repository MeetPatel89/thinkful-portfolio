Hello! My name is Meet Patel. I am an aspiring software developer based in Kansas City and currently a student at Thinkful's Engineering Immersion Course.

My Passions

To be beholden to life means doing something you love and find purpose in. I started dabbling in coding a few months back on Codecademy. What began as a mere curiosity soon morphed into a comprehensive obsession. The idea that you could solve problems by meticulously articulating the logic and writing a few lines of a language seemed too good to be true. Yet it turned out to be true day in, day out. Learning to code gave me a sense of control which I never seemed to have in my erstwhile life. Coding also complements a deep passion of mine- healthcare. The possibilities of technological frontier in healthcare and biotechnology are immense. One of the significant limitations of healthcare in its current instantiation is that it is too qualitative and crude in its application. Building a comprehensive techstack underlying healthcare systems can help convert a traditionally messy human enterprise into a more organized and analytical one.

I am also very passionate about writing. Immersing myself in creative storytelling keeps me rooted in sanity amidst chaos. I believe everything around us could be understood in context of storytelling. When told well, stories help us relate to the universe. Stories help us understand and comprehend the universe. And if it's our wish, stories help us escape the universe into some metaphorical realm of our choosing.
