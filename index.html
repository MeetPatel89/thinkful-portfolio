Hello! My name is Meet Patel. I am a software engineer based in Kansas City, Kansas.

For users who want to optimize their finances, Fin-Stack allows them to store all of their finances, stick to a budget and save money. It's that simple. They can register, log-in, enter your accounts, expenses, balance, and visualize the spending stats.

Sugar Track

For users looking to maintain a logbook of their health-metrics, Sugar-Track allows them to log metrics for glucose levels, medications, and meals and visualize them on a graphical interface. Maintaining a logbook of blood sugar levels ensures better monitoring of glycemic health.

Github Client Repo Github Server Repo Sugar Track Live

Spaced Repetition

For users willing to learn a new language, this project uses spaced repetition to allow them to register an account, efficiently learn a certain number of Gujarati (one of the many Indian languages) words from a database, keep track of their score, and the number of times they've gotten a word correct and incorrect.
